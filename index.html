Admission Criteria

Eligibility for Admission

Candidates must fulfill the following criteria for admission to undergraduate engineering programs:

  • Minimum age of 17 years by December 31st of the admission year.
  • Successful completion of Class 10+2 from HSC / CBSE / ICSE or equivalent.
  • Mandatory subjects: Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM).
  • Minimum marks as prescribed by the Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA).
